Overview

The Child Reintegration Centre is a child welfare organization in Bo, Sierra Leone focused on ensuring that vulnerable children grow up in the care of loving, safe and permanent families.  The CRC works to reintegrate children separated from parental care (primarily street-connected) back into families, provide support to children and families through case management and family strengthening programs, and to support other orphanages transitioning from residential to family-based care and reintegration through training, coaching, and mentorship consultancy.  The CRC employs 11 qualified case managers among other staff.  The CRC Director has a BA in social work.
